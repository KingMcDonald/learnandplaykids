================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Learn & Play Kids is an interactive educational game designed to make learning
fun and engaging for children. The app features a unique virtual garden system
where kids grow their own magical plants by completing educational activities
and earning points. The more they learn, the bigger their plant grows!

Key Theme: Learning through play with instant visual rewards

KEY GAMEPLAY MECHANICS
================================================================================

Game Loop:
1. Players enter their name on start screen
2. Select from available learning activities
3. Complete questions/challenges in selected activity
4. Earn points for correct answers
5. Points accumulate toward next plant stage
6. Plant grows visually when stage reaches threshold
7. Unlock new activities as player progresses
8. Session data tracked for learning analytics

Scoring System:
- Each correct answer = points
- Points accumulate toward plant growth
- Score thresholds increase per plant stage
- Progressive difficulty encourages continued play
